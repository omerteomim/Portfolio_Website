Telegram Bot for videos summary

A Telegram Bot for video summarization is an automated tool designed to receive video files from users, extract key content, and generate concise summaries. The bot interacts with users via Telegram's API, allowing them to upload videos directly. It then processes the video by extracting audio, transcribing spoken words using AI-powered speech-to-text models, and analyzing the transcript to generate a brief, meaningful summary directly within the user Telegram chat.
